# Trace

**Trace is your Mac’s local memory.**  
Search what you’ve seen — fast, private, and on-device.

## What it does
- Captures what’s visible on your screen (with your permission)
- Extracts text via OCR
- Enables fast keyword + semantic search
- Organizes recent activity into sessions

## Privacy-first
- Local-only storage
- No cloud sync
- No tracking
- No accounts

## Requirements
- macOS 14+ (recommended)
- Screen Recording permission enabled for capture

## Status
This project is under active development.
